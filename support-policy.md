## Support Scope

Support is intended for:

- help with app features and expected behavior
- troubleshooting crashes, bugs, or unexpected results
- assistance with restoring the one-time Premium unlock
- questions about the App’s free tier, Premium access, widgets, and limits

## Response Goal

Support requests are typically answered within 3 to 5 business days. Response times may be longer during weekends, holidays, or periods of high volume.

Support is provided on a reasonable-effort basis. A response is not guaranteed within a specific legal deadline unless required by applicable law.

## Premium Purchases and Refunds

TrulyRandom Premium is a one-time unlock sold through Apple’s App Store.

If you purchased Premium and it does not appear to be active, first try the App’s restore purchase option.

Refunds, billing disputes, and payment issues are generally handled by Apple through the App Store. If you need a refund, you should use Apple’s refund request process.

## Local Data and Backups

TrulyRandom primarily stores app content locally on your device, including saved custom lists and preferences.

Because of this:

- deleting the App or resetting your device may remove local app data
- recovery of deleted local data may not be possible
- support cannot guarantee restoration of custom lists or other local content unless it still exists on your device or in a device backup you control

## Device Compatibility

Support covers the current public version of the App on supported iPhone and iPad devices running a supported version of iOS or iPadOS.

Older operating systems, beta operating systems, modified devices, or unsupported environments may not be fully supported.

## What Support May Ask For

To investigate a problem, support may ask for limited troubleshooting details such as:

- your device model
- iOS version
- App version
- a short description of what happened
- screenshots or screen recordings you choose to provide

Please do not send payment card numbers, passwords, or other sensitive information.

## Feature Requests

Feature requests and product suggestions are welcome, but submission of an idea does not guarantee that it will be added to the App.

## Changes to This Policy

This Support Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version should be published with the App’s support materials, website, or App Store listing support page.
