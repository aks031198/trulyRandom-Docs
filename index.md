# TrulyRandom Docs

This site hosts the public policy pages for **TrulyRandom**.

TrulyRandom is an iOS app for generating random numbers, flipping coins, rolling dice, spinning a wheel, and choosing from custom lists. The app offers a one-time Premium unlock.

## Premium Unlock

TrulyRandom Premium is a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase sold through Apple’s App Store.

Unless a custom license agreement is shown on the App Store product page, use of the app is governed by Apple’s standard EULA.
